## 摘要
本文研究了一种基于张量的方法，用于数值求解一类定义在支持常用曲线坐标表示的空间域上的扩散-反应方程。二维中的典型示例包括圆盘（极坐标），三维中包括球体或圆柱体（球面或柱面坐标），以及涉及 Laplace-Beltrami 算子的球面问题。所提出的方法基于对 Laplace 算子精心选择的有限差分离散化，从而产生具有 Kronecker 积之和结构化表示的矩阵。在时间积分方面，我们引入了一种指数 Euler 方法的新型分裂变体，它能有效处理刚性问题，并避免了经典显式方法中严苛的时间步长限制。通过利用所得离散算子的特殊形式和所选的分裂策略，我们在 μ-模式框架下通过适当的张量-矩阵乘积来计算所需的 φ1 矩阵函数作用。我们在广泛的具有物理相关性的二维和三维耦合扩散-反应系统示例上证明了该方法的效率，这些系统生成了具有高达 10^6 个自由度的图灵模式。

## Abstract
In this paper, we study a tensor-based method for the numerical solution of a class of diffusion--reaction equations defined on spatial domains that admit common curvilinear coordinate representations. Typical examples in 2D include disks (polar coordinates), and in 3D balls or cylinders (spherical or cylindrical coordinates) as well as spheres for problems involving the Laplace--Beltrami operator. The proposed approach is based on a carefully chosen finite difference discretization of the Laplace operators that yields matrices with a structured representation as sums of Kronecker products. For the time integration, we introduce a novel split variant of the exponential Euler method that effectively handles the stiffness and avoids the severe time step size restriction of classical explicit methods. By exploiting the peculiar form of the obtained discretized operators and the chosen splitting strategy, we compute the needed action of the $\varphi_1$ matrix function through suitable tensor-matrix products in a $μ$-mode framework. We demonstrate the efficiency the approach on a wide range of physically relevant 2D and 3D examples of coupled diffusion--reaction systems generating Turing patterns with up to $10^6$ degrees of freedom.
