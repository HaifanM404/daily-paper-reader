## 摘要
本研究对使用一致导数（Conformable Derivative）和 Caputo 导数构建的分数阶时滞微分方程进行了理论与计算研究。针对一致导数情形，我们推导了相关的积分、指数函数和拉普拉斯变换，展示了一致拉普拉斯框架如何保留代数结构并促进显式解的求解。时滞项通过级数展开和基于变换的方法进行处理，确保了因果性和有限表示。与此同时，研究了基于 Caputo 的公式，强调了卷积记忆核带来的挑战以及长期数值不稳定的可能性。数值实现采用网格对齐算法：针对一致动力学采用 Euler 和 Runge-Kutta 格式，针对 Caputo 动力学采用 Euler、L2-σ 以及级数锚定预测-校正方法。对比实验表明，一致导数在解析解与数值解之间产生稳定且一致的吻合，而 Caputo 动力学则需要高阶或级数锚定格式来抑制离散噪声并保持长期精度。这些结果强调了一致形式在模拟具有时滞和记忆的动态现象方面的优势，为基于积分的分数阶模型提供了一种易于处理且具有物理可解释性的替代方案。

## Abstract
This work presents an analytical and computational study of fractional-order delay differential equations formulated using both the conformable and Caputo derivatives. For the conformable case, we develop the associated integral, exponential function, and Laplace transform, showing how the conformable Laplace framework preserves algebraic structure and facilitates explicit solutions. Delay terms are treated through series expansions and transform-based methods, ensuring causal and finite representations. In parallel, Caputo-based formulations are examined, highlighting the challenges posed by convolutional memory kernels and the potential for long-term numerical instability. Numerical implementations are carried out using mesh-aligned algorithms: Euler and Runge--Kutta schemes for conformable dynamics, and Euler, L2--$σ$, and a series--anchored predictor--corrector method for Caputo dynamics. Comparative experiments demonstrate that conformable derivatives yield stable, consistent agreement between analytic and numerical solutions, whereas Caputo dynamics require higher-order or series-anchored schemes to suppress discretization noise and maintain long-term accuracy. These results underscore the advantages of the conformable formalism in modeling dynamic phenomena with delay and memory, offering a tractable and physically interpretable alternative to integral-based fractional models.
