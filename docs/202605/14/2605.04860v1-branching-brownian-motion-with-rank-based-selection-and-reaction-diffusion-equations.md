## 摘要
我们考虑一类分支-选择粒子系统，其中粒子以随时间变化的速率 $r$ 进行分支，并根据其排名通过某个函数 $ψ$ 以一定的概率被杀死。我们证明了在相当宽松的条件下，此类系统的流体动力学极限由反应扩散方程 $U_t = \frac12 U_{xx} + r(t)G(U)$ 给出，其中非线性项 $G(U)$ 是关于 $ψ$ 的函数。这是对已被广泛研究的 $N$-BBM 过程的重大推广，并且类似于 Groisman 和 Soprano-Loto (arXiv:2008.09460) 所描述的“$(b,D)$-BBM”过程族。一方面，这使我们能够将常见的反应扩散方程理解为具有简单描述的相互作用粒子系统的极限；另一方面，反应扩散偏微分方程（PDE）解的渐近行为可以帮助我们预测相关粒子系统的渐近性质。我们给出了分支-选择粒子系统具有渐近速度的一般条件，并描述了该速度直至 $(\log N)^{-2}$ 阶的项；此外，我们还描述了该速度与相应反应扩散方程的传播速度和行波解之间的联系。这提供了一个部分弱选择原理。

## Abstract
We consider a family of branching-selection particle systems in which particles branch at time dependent rate $r$ and are killed with a probability which is dependent on their rank via some function $ψ$. We show that, under fairly minimal conditions, the hydrodynamic limit of such a system is given by the reaction-diffusion equation $U_t = \frac12 U_{xx} + r(t)G(U)$ with nonlinearity $G(U)$ which is a function of $ψ$. This is a significant generalisation of the well-studied $N$-BBM process, and is similar to the family of `$(b,D)$-BBM' processes described by Groisman \& Soprano-Loto (arXiv:2008.09460). On the one hand, this allows us to understand common reaction-diffusion equations as limits of interacting particle systems with simple descriptions. On the other hand, the asymptotic behaviour of solutions of the reaction-diffusion PDEs can help us predict the asymptotic properties of the associated particle systems. We give general conditions under which the branching-selection particle system has an asymptotic velocity, and describe the velocity up to order $(\log N)^{-2}$; furthermore, we describe the connection between this velocity and the spreading speeds and travelling waves of the corresponding reaction-diffusion equation. This provides a partial weak selection principle.
